Me(Prathamjeet Singh) along with my group members Shivam Gusain, Akshat Gupta & Shourya P Singh created this project as our final year project under the guidance of Mr. Ravi Anand. Our main objective of this project was to develop a system that suggests movies to users based on their preferences and the content of the movies themselves. The primary goal was to provide personalised movie recommendation to user, taking into account their individual tastes, interests, and viewing history.
